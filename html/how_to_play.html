HOW TO PLAY

CONTROLS

  • W, A, S, D MOVE
  • MOUSE RIGHT CLICK SHOOT
  • R RELOAD
  • SPACE DASH
  • E INTERACT (Refill Gas, pick up items, etc.)
  • P PAUSE

OBJECTIVE

Try to fill your car's (orange) gas tank by scavenging fuel from abandoned cars.

Survive against 4 different types of enemies that will try to stop you.

Shoot them before they get you!
